About Us

ABOUT OF FunZone Fantasy Amusement Park

FunZone Fantasy Amusement Park is a magical destination where dreams come to life and adventures await around every corner. Nestled between rolling hills and sparkling rivers, this enchanting park offers a unique blend of thrilling rides, whimsical attractions, and immersive experiences designed for visitors of all ages.

Our Services

Food Court

The food court at FunZone Fantasy is a vibrant and diverse area designed to cater to a wide range of tastes and preferences.

Hunted House

The Haunted House at FunZone Fantasy is a thrilling and immersive experience designed to scare and entertain visitors of all ages. It features a blend of classic horror elements and modern special effects, creating an unforgettable adventure through a world of ghosts, ghouls, and spine-chilling surprises.

Jewellery Stalls

The jewelry stalls at FunZone Fantasy offer a dazzling array of accessories, from elegant pieces to whimsical designs, perfect for visitors looking to take home a special memento. Each stall has its own unique theme and style, ensuring there’s something for everyone.
